Laboratory and Pathology Services

-> Haematology: Precise testing and management of blood disorders such as anemia, clotting abnormalities, and leukemia. Our haematology lab ensures accurate and timely results.

-> Biochemistry: Comprehensive testing to diagnose and monitor metabolic conditions, including diabetes, liver disorders, and kidney function.

-> Microbiology: Advanced pathogen identification to diagnose bacterial, viral, and fungal infections. Our microbiology lab supports targeted treatment strategies.

-> Serology: Testing for antibodies and antigens to diagnose infections and assess immune response, ensuring timely and effective care.

-> Histopathology: Detailed examination of tissue samples for early and accurate diagnosis of cancers and other diseases.

-> Cytology: Precise cell analysis to detect abnormalities, including early signs of cancer and other diseases, aiding in prompt treatment.

-> Clinical Pathology: Full-spectrum diagnostic services for blood, urine, and other bodily fluids to aid in the diagnosis of various conditions.

-> Hormones & Immunoassays: Specialized tests for hormonal and immune disorders, helping to diagnose thyroid conditions, infertility, and more.
