Our Key Tests

We offer a wide range of specialized tests to ensure precise diagnosis and effective treatment:

AIDS Testing

We provide confidential and accurate testing for HIV/AIDS using advanced diagnostic methods. Our center ensures patient privacy and delivers timely results with counseling and support for better management.

Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care, including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. Our skilled dentists specialize in treatments such as teeth cleaning, cavity filling, root canal therapy, dental implants, and orthodontics. We prioritize oral health and aesthetics in a comfortable environment.

HMPV Testing

Advanced diagnostic testing for Human Metapneumovirus infections, a leading cause of respiratory illnesses. Our precise and reliable testing methods enable early detection, aiding in effective treatment and recovery.

Dengue Testing

Accurate and timely testing for Dengue fever using advanced diagnostic methods like NS1 antigen, IgM, and IgG antibody tests. Our services ensure early detection, helping in effective management and prevention of complications.
